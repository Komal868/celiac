Gluten-Free Diet for Celiac

✅ Safe to Eat (Gluten-Free Foods)

  • ✅ Rice (Chawal)
  • ✅ Besan (Gram Flour)
  • ✅ Bajra, Jowar, Ragi (Millets)
  • ✅ Fruits & Vegetables
  • ✅ Milk, Paneer, Dahi (Dairy)
  • ✅ Eggs, Meat, Chicken, Fish (Natural form)
  • ✅ Makki ka atta (Corn flour)
  • ✅ Gluten-free labeled oats (Certified)

❌ Avoid These (Contain Gluten)

  • ❌ Gehun (Wheat) – Atta, Maida, Suji
  • ❌ Barley (Jau)
  • ❌ Rye (rare in India)
  • ❌ Bread, Pasta, Pizza base (normal)
  • ❌ Noodles, Biscuits (unless labeled gluten-free)
  • ❌ Cakes, Pastries, Maida-based snacks
  • ❌ Soya Sauce (often has wheat)

💡 Tips for Indian Homes

✔️ Use separate utensils (belan, tawa) to avoid gluten cross-contamination. ✔️ Read packaged food labels carefully. ✔️ Buy only “gluten-free” certified atta. ✔️ Prefer home-cooked food over outside items.
