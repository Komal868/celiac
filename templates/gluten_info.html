Understanding gluten and how it affects people with Celiac Disease or gluten sensitivity.

üîç What is Gluten?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. It gives dough its elasticity and helps it rise and keep its shape. But for some people, gluten can cause serious health problems.

üö´ Why Avoid Gluten?

People with Celiac Disease experience an immune reaction that damages the small intestine when they eat gluten. Others may have non-celiac gluten sensitivity, causing digestive discomfort and fatigue.

‚ùì FAQs

What is Celiac Disease?

Celiac Disease is an autoimmune condition in which eating gluten leads to damage in the small intestine.

Is gluten bad for everyone?

No. Most people can consume gluten without any issues. It's mainly harmful to those with Celiac Disease or gluten sensitivity.

Can I eat oats?

Pure oats are gluten-free but are often contaminated during processing. Look for certified gluten-free oats.
