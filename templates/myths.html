🚫 Myth Buster - Celiac Disease

❌ Myth: Gluten allergy is the same as celiac disease

✅ Fact: Celiac is an autoimmune disorder, not just an allergy.

❌ Myth: Only children get celiac disease

✅ Fact: Celiac can develop at any age—even in older adults.

❌ Myth: You can outgrow celiac disease

✅ Fact: It is a lifelong condition; only managed through diet.

❌ Myth: A little gluten won't hurt

✅ Fact: Even tiny amounts can trigger damage.

❌ Myth: Celiac disease is rare

✅ Fact: Affects approx 1 in 100 people worldwide.

❌ Myth: You must have stomach issues

✅ Fact: Symptoms vary—fatigue, anemia, or even none.
