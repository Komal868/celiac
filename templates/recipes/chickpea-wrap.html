Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 boiled potato
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, chili)
  • Rice flour (for binding)
  • Gluten-free roti or wrap
  • Chutney & veggies

Steps:

  1. Mash chickpeas and potato together.
  2. Add spices and a spoon of rice flour to form a tikki.
  3. Shallow fry until golden brown.
  4. Place the tikki in a gluten-free wrap with chutney, veggies, and roll it.
  5. Enjoy with mint chutney!
