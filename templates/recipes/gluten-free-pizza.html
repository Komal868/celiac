🍕 Gluten-Free Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 gluten-free pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Chopped veggies: capsicum, olives, tomatoes, corn
  • Oregano, chili flakes

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Spread sauce over the gluten-free base.
  3. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until cheese melts.
