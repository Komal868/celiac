🍲 Veggie Quinoa Soup

🥦 Ingredients:

  • Quinoa (½ cup)
  • Carrots, Beans, Peas, Corn
  • Garlic & Onion
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Salt, Pepper, Herbs

🧑‍🍳 Steps:

1. Sauté onion & garlic in a pan.
2. Add veggies and stir-fry for 3–4 mins.
3. Add quinoa and broth. Cook until quinoa is soft.
4. Add salt, pepper, herbs and serve warm.
