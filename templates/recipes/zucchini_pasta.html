🥒 Zucchini Pasta

📝 Ingredients:

  • Zucchini (spiralized)
  • Olive oil
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions:

1. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
2. Add cherry tomatoes and cook till soft.
3. Toss in spiralized zucchini and cook for 2–3 mins.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Top with parmesan and serve warm.
