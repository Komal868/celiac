🧪 How is Celiac Disease Diagnosed?

Celiac disease ka diagnosis blood tests aur biopsy ke zariye hota hai. Agar aapko symptoms hain (like pet dard, weight loss, weakness), to doctor test recommend karta hai.

🔬 Common Tests:

  • tTG-IgA Test: Blood test to check antibodies
  • Endoscopy with Biopsy: Chhoti aant se tissue sample lekar test kiya jata hai
  • Genetic Test: To check HLA-DQ2/DQ8 genes (optional)

❗ Important:

Blood test se pehle aapko **gluten khana chhodna nahi chahiye**, warna result false aa sakta hai.

📞 Doctor se Kab Milna Chahiye?

Agar aapko frequently loose motions, stomach bloating, iron deficiency, ya unexplained weakness ho rahi hai — please ek gastroenterologist se consult karein.
